### PART A — IDENTITY AND BACKGROUND

1. I am the registered operator of motor vehicle with registration number **[VEHICLE REGO]**.

2. I am the full-time primary carer for my mother, **Cheryl Bruce-Sanders** (Date of Birth: 11 September 1947), who resides with me at the above address.

3. My mother has been diagnosed with **Cerebral Amyloid Angiopathy (CAA)**, a progressive neurological condition requiring 24-hour care. I have been her sole carer without respite for over 90 consecutive days.

4. I have **chronic kidney disease** which further limits my capacity to attend in-person appointments or government offices.

---

### PART B — REGISTRATION CANCELLATION ATTEMPT

5. On or about **[DATE OF EMAIL]**, I sent an email to the Department of Transport and Main Roads (TMR) requesting cancellation of the registration for vehicle **[VEHICLE REGO]**.

6. The email was sent to the address **[EMAIL ADDRESS USED]**, which I believed at the time to be the correct TMR address for registration cancellations.

7. I subsequently received an automated bounce-back notification from the email server with the error message: **"550 5.1.1 User Unknown"** — indicating the email address I used was not a valid or active TMR address.

8. I was not informed by TMR of the correct email address for registration cancellations. The address I used was obtained from publicly available information.

9. As a result of the email bounce, my registration cancellation was not processed, and the vehicle remained registered without my knowledge or intent.

10. I was unable to attend a TMR service centre in person due to my full-time caring responsibilities for my mother and my own health condition.

### PART D — GROUNDS FOR REVIEW

14. I respectfully submit that the above fines should be reviewed and withdrawn on the following grounds:
